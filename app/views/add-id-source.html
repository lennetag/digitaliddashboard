Why connect additional ID sources?

Connecting additional ID sources to your Digital ID allows you to:

  • Share more specific information when needed
  • Access services that require particular credentials
  • Reduce the need to repeatedly provide documents
  • Keep all your verified identity information in one secure place

You remain in control of what information is shared and when. Data is only accessed from these sources when you explicitly authorize it.
