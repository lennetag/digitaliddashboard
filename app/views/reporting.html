Roadmap & Future Development

Key milestones and future development plans for the Digital ID service. This roadmap represents our current strategic direction and may evolve based on user needs and government priorities.

âœ“ Completed (Q1-Q3 2024)

Phase 1: Core Identity & Age Verification

  • Age verification - Over 18 and Under 18 checks with privacy-preserving options
  • Identity verification - Full identity confirmation with configurable data sharing
  • Enhanced verification - Live photo capture for high-confidence identity checks
  • Temporary code generation - Time-limited, single-use verification codes
  • QR code scanning - In-person verification via mobile devices
  • GOV.UK One Login integration - Seamless connection to existing government identity system

ðŸ”¨ In Progress (Q4 2024)

Phase 2: Connected Services & Private Sector Expansion

  • Connected ID sources management - User interface to view, add, and remove ID sources (Passport, Driving Licence, Visas)
  • Address confirmation verification - Prove current address from driving licence or electoral roll
  • Right to Work checks - Instant employment eligibility verification for employers
  • Private sector pilot - Banking and financial services integration trials
  • Mobile app development - Native iOS and Android applications

ðŸ“… Planned Q1-Q2 2025

Phase 3: Extended Verification Services

  • Driving licence status verification - Check driving eligibility, points, and endorsements
  • NHS employment verification - Confirm employment at NHS trusts and organizations
  • Veteran identity confirmation - Verify military service and access veteran benefits
  • Company director verification - Prove directorship status via Companies House
  • Student identity verification - Confirm student status for discounts and services
  • Biometric enhancements - Facial recognition and liveness detection improvements

ðŸ”® Future (Q3 2025 onwards)

Phase 4: Comprehensive Identity Ecosystem

  • Visa type and validity checks - Immigration status verification for employers and landlords
  • Benefit recipient status - Confirm eligibility for social benefits and support
  • Vehicle ownership verification - Prove vehicle ownership via DVLA
  • DBS clearance checks - Disclosure and Barring Service status verification
  • Maternity exemption status - Healthcare cost exemption confirmation
  • Professional qualifications - Verify teaching, medical, and professional certifications
  • International interoperability - EU Digital Identity Wallet integration
  • Blockchain anchoring - Decentralized verification proof storage
  • Zero-knowledge proofs - Advanced privacy-preserving verification methods
  • Delegated access - Allow trusted representatives to act on behalf of users
